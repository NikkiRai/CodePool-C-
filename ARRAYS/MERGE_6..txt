   STEP 1:Negative elements of A are occupied on left
          side of C from left to right.
   STEP 2:Negative elments of B are occupied onto C
          after the negative elements of A.
   STEP 3:Positive elements of A are picked up from left to right
          and occupied onto the right side of C.
   STEP 4: Positive elements of B are picked up in left to right 
           manner and occupied on C on the right side.
